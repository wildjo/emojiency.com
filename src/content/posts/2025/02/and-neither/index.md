<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I used to believe<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;men were mean<br>women were wise<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and I was alone and neither</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>When we were young<br>     we laid upon<br>the bed instead of<br>     in it, together</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I used to believe<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I knew my needs<br>my needs were mine<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and that was it</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And then I found<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;my needs were not<br>the only needs,<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;that mattered</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And then I knew<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;what makes men wise<br>and women mean<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and why I felt alone</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>and neither</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
