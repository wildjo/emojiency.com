SplatWorld has a new name—Worldsimworld—and Claude Fable 5 and I made remarkable progress today. What began as a chunked torus is turning into a navigable procedural planet with rolling terrain, a sun and moon, stars, rings, and neighboring worlds.

It is beginning to feel less like a technical test and more like a place.
