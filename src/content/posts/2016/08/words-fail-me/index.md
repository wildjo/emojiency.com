I'm editing a cement pumper into an image of the Volunteer Park reservoir. The vehicle fits best into the scene if it's flipped horizontally. That's fine, not a problem. [Flip Horizontal] at your service.



Just one problem.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-772" src="http://emojiency.com/bloggityblogblog/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/flipping_text.png" alt="flipping_text" width="1341" height="705" />

Select the text using the Magic Wand brush (really pales in comparison to Pixelmator's selection brush, but it's the best Adobe could do in a pinch), Rubber Stamp the green back into the armature, a little rotate and a little skew and the text is readable again. Back it goes to make the pumper arm appear <em>au naturel</em>.

Still more work to do on this one. Editing around the cement mixer and pumper is more work than I thought it would be. I can't wait until I get to the windscreen on the cement pumper, that's going to be a treat, but I think I know exactly how to deal with it.

(Famous last words, right there.)
