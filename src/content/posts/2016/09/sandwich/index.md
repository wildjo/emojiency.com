A sandwich from <a href="http://theliveinkitchen.com/2012/02/24/ultimate-turkey-sandwich/">Duckduckgo image search</a>, traced and colored in <a href="http://www.pixelmator.com/ios/">Pixelmator</a>.



Pixelmator for iOS is amazing. I feel like in many ways it's more powerful and fun than even Procreate. I really love how Procreate lets me rotate the canvas and I miss that in Pixelmator, but Pixelmator lets me do more on the canvas, it feels like.

Having many tools and being able to switch between them makes me happy. Having a wealth of drawing apps that work well on the iPad Pro and with Apple Pencil is a problem I am happy to have.
