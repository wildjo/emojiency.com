Seattle International Narcotics Anonymous Convention (SINAC) 2017 had an opening for a graphic designer. I'm volunteering to do the work and this is my introduction piece. It combines a variation on the Seattle Mariner's logo with the organization's logo, spelling out the SINAC acronym.



I adjusted the color scheme from the teal, navy and gray of the Mariner's to the green, gold and white of the organization.

<img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-2082" src="http://emojiency.com/bloggityblogblog/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/Screen-Shot-2016-09-12-at-11.01.43-AM-1024x751.png" alt="screen-shot-2016-09-12-at-11-01-43-am" width="474" height="348" />

It almost certainly can't be used on any of the merchandise for the convention, because lawyers. That's okay: it's a cute remix, a bit of fun on a Monday morning, and can be used internally.

Finally, I made the NA logo partially transparent and slightly rotated to better show the now-finished compass points.
