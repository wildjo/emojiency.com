Our bowling team needed a logo for shirts, because bowling teams need shirts and shirts need logos. I don't make the rules, I just break them when I can.



I used a <a href="http://patione.deviantart.com/art/The-XXI-Century-Buddha-336786360">Chibi Buddha that I found on Deviant Art</a> <a href="http://patione.deviantart.com">by Patione</a> as a reference. I hope it doesn't come off as too plagiarized. I used the pen tool in Affinity Designer to draw the lines. The brush tool and I aren't friends yet, but we're working on it.

Gutter Monks type is Chalkduster from macOS (that looks so weird after years of typing Mac OS X).
