<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>O’ how I wish<br>the shaking would cease<br>insecurity’s siblings:<br>Doubt and Misgiving<br>go away now and<br>leave me in peace</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>O’ how it pulses<br>the heartbeat inside<br>Adrenaline rising<br>my self I’m despising<br>please let me finish<br>diminish, denied</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Were I alone<br>upon this bright stage<br>I’d be calm and free<br>not afraid to be me<br>and breathe life the bare words<br>affixed upon this pale and flimsy page</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>What trauma befell me<br>Before memory’s past<br>what torture session<br>suspended expression<br>of pure and honest self<br>before the curtain close at last</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Did it happen to you too?<br>The accident that on purpose befell<br>The event that defied<br>being forgotten or denied?<br>The anyhow that ever I could<br>forgive of you, but never myself</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The me I once loved<br>still quaked and shook,<br>and survived — not just<br>that, but thrived —<br>feared not looking foolish<br>Chances taken: advances took</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>O’ how I wish<br>the shaking would assuage<br>That I would know my spirit again<br>undiminished by my weary pain<br>And leave me be in peace<br>when next I grace this stage.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Jonnie Wilder, read at Work in Progress night, Vermillion 2019</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
